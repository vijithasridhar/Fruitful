A 10 minute monthly checkup can...

  • increase your chance of a positive outcome from breast cancer by 15-20%
  • prevent skin cancer and have a 98% chance of optimistic results

Rates are on the rise:
Save the Peaches!

  • Over 200,000 new cases of breast cancer diagnosed in 2015 and 40,000 deaths.
  • About 1 in 8 U.S. women (about 12%) will develop invasive breast cancer over the course of her lifetime.
  • Breast self-exams have increased biopsy rate by 50%

Sunscreen is not enough:
Beware of the berry!

  • Over 80,000 new cases in 2015 and 13,000 deaths.
  • 1 in 5 Americans will develop skin cancer in his/her lifetime.
  • If caught early, early stage melanoma has a five year survival rate of 98%.

Be a baller:
Check your cherries!

  • 1 in 2,000 men will develop testicular cancer in his lifetime.
  • If caught early, testicular cancer has a 99% survival rate
